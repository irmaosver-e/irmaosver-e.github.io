    You can apply the patch using this online tool: https://www.romhacking.net/patch/

    Why “NBA Jam Tournament Edition”?

    I’ll admit, I’m not usually a fan of sports games. But after wrapping up my last project, Eliminate Down Remix, I was looking for a quick challenge. That’s when SpkLeader reached out and asked me to take a look at NBA Jam TE, which uses EEPROM saving instead of SRAM—since lower-tier flash carts don’t support EEPROM saves. I thought, “Well, this could be interesting and would allow more people access to the game with its saving features, I'll have a go at it!

    Hack features:

    • SEGA logo palette fix, corrects the logo outline from light blue to white
    • Save system conversion from EEPROM to SRAM, allows for lower-tier flash carts to save the game
