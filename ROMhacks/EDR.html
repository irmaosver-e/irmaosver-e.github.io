    Why “Eliminate Down”?

    While playing the game, I thought, “This is great—but wouldn’t it be even better if I could use the 6-button controller to switch weapons and adjust speed?”
    Also, continues felt pointless — I’d restart a level with no power-ups, so I might as well start over and not die at all.
    That’s when I realised: I could fix this!

    Hack features:

    • A brand-new Remix mode accessible via the options menu
    • Cleared stages become selectable from the menu.
    • A speed cycle function for 3-button controllers.
    • Weapon and speed control for 6-button controllers.
    • The mini-game has been updated to grant credits and unlock cheats
    • Progression and scores saved to SRAM at the transition from game over to the SEGA logo.
